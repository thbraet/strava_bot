Automatically hide short activities from your Strava feed

How it works:

  1. Connect your Strava account
  2. Set your preferred time thresholds for different activities
  3. We'll automatically hide short activities from your feed
  4. Activities remain public but won't clutter your friends' feeds

Why use this app?

If you're like many Strava users, you may track all your activities but only want to share significant workouts with your followers.

This tool automatically flags shorter activities as "Hide from Feed" so they remain public but don't appear in your followers' feed.

No more manually adjusting privacy settings for each activity!
